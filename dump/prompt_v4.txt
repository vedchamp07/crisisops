FIX 1 — Restore deception detection by removing the early-gather system
prompt instruction and enabling re-verification

FILE: baselines/llm_agent.py

Part A — Revert the system prompt STEP A change.
Find the block that starts with:
  "IMPORTANT: By the time you receive your first turn, ALL team members
  have already been cross-verified..."

Replace it with the original gather instruction:
  "STEP A — GATHER (FREE, costs no budget):
   After your first turn, some members may still be unverified. If any
   team member has NOT been cross-verified yet, call
   query_observable_signals for them — this is always valid. However,
   after 2 consecutive free queries you MUST take a paid action before
   querying again."

Rationale: signals gathered at step 1-2 have ticket_age=0-1 days,
below the 3-day detection threshold. The LLM must be allowed to
re-verify at step 8+ when ticket_age rises to 4+ days and deception
becomes detectable.

Part B — In _get_forced_action, change the communicate condition.
Current: "if step > team_size and communicate not in actions_taken"
Change to: "if step > team_size + 2 and communicate not in actions_taken"

Rationale: with 3-member teams, step > 3 fires communicate at step 4.
The LLM only gets step 5 onward. Adding a 2-step buffer (step > 5)
gives the LLM at least one turn before the forced communicate fires,
so it can do re-verification at step 5 when ticket_age=2+ days.

Part C — Lower the deceptive_commits detection threshold.
Current:
  deceptive_commits = (reported > _DECEPTION_REPORT_THRESHOLD
                       and commits == 0
                       and ticket_age > 0)
Change the commits check from == 0 to <= 1:
  deceptive_commits = (reported > _DECEPTION_REPORT_THRESHOLD
                       and commits <= 1   # FIX-1: was ==0, low-vel members produce commits=1
                       and ticket_age > 0)

Also lower _DECEPTION_TICKET_AGE_DAYS from 3 to 2:
  _DECEPTION_TICKET_AGE_DAYS = 2  # FIX-1: was 3, detects at step 4 not step 6

Rationale: VELOCITY_LOW = 0.05-0.25. At velocity 0.10-0.25, commits =
round(v * 6) = 1. The rule with commits==0 only fires for velocity≤0.05
(bottom 5% of deceptive members). With <= 1 it fires for velocity≤0.25
(all deceptive members). Ticket age > 2 means detection fires at step 5
(current_step=4, days=4//2=2), which is after the forced gather.

FIX 4 — Fix the fallback early-submit (documented in CLAUDE.md)

FILE: baselines/llm_agent.py
LOCATION: _fallback_action method, the "Nothing actionable — submit" block

Current logic: after one reassign + one escalate, submits immediately.
This exits at step 7 of 30. Tasks complete at ~step 23. Submitting early
means recovery_pct ≈ 0 → LLM scores ~0.31 vs greedy ~0.45.

Replace the final "Nothing actionable — submit" block with a loop that
keeps reassigning crisis tasks to members as tasks complete:

    # FIX-4: keep reassigning until budget is low — don't submit early
    # Find any unresolved crisis task not yet on the most available member
    for crisis in unresolved:
        for task_id in crisis.get("affected_task_ids", []):
            # Find current holder
            current_holder = next(
                (m["member_id"] for m in team
                 if task_id in m.get("assigned_task_ids", [])),
                None,
            )
            # Find best available non-deceptive member
            candidates = [m for m in team
                         if m["member_id"] not in deceptive_ids
                         and m.get("reported_availability", 0) > 0.3]
            if not candidates:
                candidates = team  # fallback: any member
            best = max(candidates, key=lambda m: m.get("reported_availability", 0))
            if current_holder != best["member_id"]:
                reassign_key = f"{task_id}:{best['member_id']}"
                attempted = self._memory.get("fallback_reassigns", set())
                if reassign_key not in attempted:
                    attempted.add(reassign_key)
                    self._memory["fallback_reassigns"] = attempted
                    self._memory["actions_taken"].append("reassign_task")
                    return {"action_type": "reassign_task",
                            "params": {"task_id": task_id,
                                       "to_member_id": best["member_id"]}}

    # Truly nothing left: communicate if overdue, then submit
    acts = self._memory["actions_taken"]
    steps_since_comm = next(
        (i for i, a in enumerate(reversed(acts)) if a == "communicate"),
        len(acts)
    )
    if steps_since_comm >= 5:
        self._memory["actions_taken"].append("communicate")
        return {"action_type": "communicate",
                "params": {"message_type": "proactive_escalation_with_plan",
                           "content": "Status update during recovery.",
                           "target": "both"}}

    self._memory["actions_taken"].append("submit_recovery_plan")
    summary = (f"Fallback submit. Deceptive: {list(deceptive_ids)}. "
               f"Unresolved: {[c['crisis_id'] for c in unresolved]}.")
    return {"action_type": "submit_recovery_plan",
            "params": {"plan_summary": summary}}
